Our story

Started local. Built to last.

Blackbird Projects was founded on a simple frustration: too many residential builds were being rushed, under-communicated, and handed over with defects that were treated as an afterthought. We wanted to do it differently.

Based in Toowoomba, we focus on a deliberate range of residential work — custom homes, renovations, bathrooms, and ongoing property care. Our team is small by design. Small enough that every project gets real attention from someone who will be on site every day.

We work across Toowoomba and the broader Darling Downs, drawing on local knowledge that a larger builder simply can't replicate — the trades, the climate, the way families in this region actually live in their homes.

What drives us

Three things we never compromise on.

01

Craft

We care about how things are built — the joints, the reveals, the material transitions that most people won't consciously notice but will always feel. That attention doesn't switch off when a project gets busy.

02

Clarity

Building a home is one of the most significant things you'll do. You deserve to know what's happening, why, and when. We communicate proactively — not just when something goes wrong.

03

Community

We are Toowoomba locals building for Toowoomba families. We use local trades where we can, we understand the region, and we're invested in the quality of homes in this community for the long run.

The team

People who care about the work they put their name to.

"Every project is a chance to build something that will outlast the conversation that started it."

Our lead builder brings over fifteen years of residential construction experience across Toowoomba and the Darling Downs. He has worked across every project type we offer — from full custom builds to detailed bathroom renovations — and is on site for every Blackbird project, not just at the beginning.

The wider team is drawn from trusted local trades with long-standing relationships built on shared standards. We don't use subcontractors we wouldn't want working in our own homes.
