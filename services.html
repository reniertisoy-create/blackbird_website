Our process

A steady process makes for a calm build.

We follow a clear four-stage process on every project — so you always know what's happening and why.

01

Initial consultation

We meet on site or over a call to understand your project, your budget, and what success looks like to you. No obligation, no pressure.

02

Planning & design

We work through drawings, specifications, and council requirements. We keep things clear and flag any decisions early so there are no surprises later.

03

The build

Regular site updates, proactive communication, and a build environment that respects your time and your property. We treat your home as if it were ours.

04

Handover & care

A thorough defect walkthrough, full documentation handover, and an ongoing relationship. The job isn't done at the keys — it's done when you're settled.
